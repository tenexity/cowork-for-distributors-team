## How to use this artifact

Read it Friday afternoons before you write your weekly review. Read it before you say yes to any new commitment that wasn't in scope on day one. Read it before any branch leadership call. The point isn't to print it once and forget — it's to come back to it often enough that the three priorities stay loud.

When something changes (a major win, a real surprise, a priority becomes irrelevant), revise the document. Don't pretend the original version is still right. The 90-day priorities are a moving target; the document should reflect that.
