## Drafted communications this week

For each account in the top two quadrants, draft the email or call script in your voice. Use `RESOURCES/TEMPLATES/email-formats-template.md` for collection-letter formats. Save drafts to `WORK AREAS/Finance/[customer]-collection-project/outputs/`.

Quick reference for tone:

- **First reminder (30-60 days):** factual, friendly, short. "Just a heads up your account shows $X past due. Need help with anything on our side?"
- **Second reminder (60-90 days):** firmer, specific. "Looking at your account at $X past due, with the oldest invoice from [date]. Can you give me a date when this clears?"
- **Pre-credit-hold (90+ days):** transparent about what's next. "If we don't see payment by [date], we'll need to hold orders pending resolution. Let's avoid that — what's the path?"
- **Credit hold notice:** clear, professional, leaves the door open. "Effective [date], your account is on credit hold. To restart, we need [amount] cleared. Call me directly to work out timing."

## How to use this artifact

Refresh Monday morning by pulling the latest AR aging from the ERP. Re-rank accounts. Action the top-right quadrant first. Use Friday afternoon to capture decisions made this week and any movement on the matrix.

Over time, watch for patterns: are the same accounts cycling into the top-right week after week? That's a credit decision waiting to be made. Are you putting too many small balances in the top-right out of frustration? That's bandwidth being misallocated.
