## Drift signals to watch (per account, when relevant)

When you see these, the account moves to "drifting" or "at risk":

- Order count down 20%+ vs. last quarter, no obvious reason
- Last meaningful contact was over 60 days ago
- They've started sourcing X category from a competitor (you hear it from a rep, a contractor, or counter)
- Payment terms slipping (was Net 30, now consistently Net 45+)
- Decision-maker change at the customer's company
- A specific sales line going to zero where it used to be steady

## How to use this artifact

Update it Friday afternoons (15 minutes — pull GP $ from the ERP, update last contact, refresh next planned contact). Use it Monday morning to plan your week's account work. Refer to it before any branch leadership call when accounts come up.

When an account hits "drifting" or "at risk," the next planned contact gets bumped to **this week** and the action gets concrete. Sliding accounts only get worse with attention deferred.
