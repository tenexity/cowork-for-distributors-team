# Weekly review template

A structured end-of-week review for distributors. Pulls together the operational week — what shipped, what didn't, what's blocking, what matters next week. Spend 20-30 minutes on Friday afternoon or Monday morning. Fill it in yourself, or ask Claude to compile it from your ERP exports, calendar, and memory logs.

## How to use this

**Option 1: Auto-compile with Claude.** Say: "Run my weekly review for the week ending [date]." If you've connected Gmail and Calendar, and you paste this week's ERP exports, Claude can draft the review from data. You edit and add the judgement calls.

**Option 2: Fill it in yourself.** Spend 20-30 minutes on Friday. The reflection is the point — even without automated data.

**Option 3: Recurring scheduled task.** Ask Claude to set up a Friday 4 PM scheduled task that pulls the operating numbers and preps the review shell. You finish it before you leave.

---

## Notes

- **The operating numbers at the top are non-negotiable.** If you skip them, the review is just commentary. Numbers anchor the narrative.
- **"What didn't get done" is the most valuable section.** Patterns here reveal systemic issues — a vendor chronically missing allocation, a branch chronically short-staffed, a customer chronically requiring hand-holding.
- **Keep it under one page printed.** Longer than that and you won't read back past reviews when you need them.
- **Save reviews to `WORK AREAS/Admin-PA/weekly-reviews-project/outputs/`** with the naming convention: `Weekly-Review_YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
- **Over time, reviews feed the System Review skill.** Run the System Review monthly and it reads patterns across 4-5 weekly reviews to surface things you'd miss week to week.
