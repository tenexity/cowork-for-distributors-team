# Customer-Accounts

Where the relationship with the contractor base lives. Key-account QBRs, carry-line reviews, customer onboarding, newsletters, contractor events, and the running record of who matters to your business.

---

## What goes here

- **Top-25 (or top-50, top-100) account management.** Quarterly Business Reviews, carry-line reviews, executive summary prep, account plans, renewal and retention strategies.
- **Customer onboarding.** Credit app collection, terms negotiation, welcome packs, first-order support, branch introductions.
- **Customer newsletters and comms.** Seasonal updates, new-product launches, price-change notifications, branch news, contractor appreciation events.
- **Contractor events.** Contractor breakfasts, vendor-sponsored training events, customer appreciation nights, branch anniversaries.
- **Loyalty and rebate programs for customers.** Purchase incentive tracking, contractor rewards, volume rebate programs you run.
- **Customer segmentation.** A/B/C customer classification, churn signals, win-back campaigns.

---

## What doesn't go here

- One-off quote work → `Sales-Ops/`
- Credit hold decisions or AR collections → `Finance/`
- Vendor-side QBRs → `Procurement-Vendors/`
- A customer asking about a specific SKU's availability → that's a quick task, `Admin-PA/`

---

## Typical projects

- `Top-25-QBR-Cycle-Q2_project/` — rolling cycle of your largest accounts
- `Acme-Plumbing-Account-Plan_project/` — a deep-dive on a single strategic account
- `Spring-Newsletter-2026_project/` — seasonal customer communication
- `Contractor-Appreciation-Night_project/` — a recurring event with planning, invite list, logistics, post-event follow-up

## The QBR cadence

Most distributors run QBRs with their top accounts quarterly, but the rhythm matters:

- **Q1 (Jan-Mar):** Annual review + year-ahead planning. Carry list changes, contract renewals.
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun):** Spring HVAC season check-in. Fill rate, back-order frequency, rep coverage.
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep):** Peak season performance review. Margin, service levels, competitive threats.
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec):** Year-end wrap, rebate payout, next-year commitment conversations.

The System Review skill will flag if your QBR cadence slips — it's a leading indicator of churn risk.
